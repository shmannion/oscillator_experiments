# Tapping data for 120bpm

In all directories, the following naming convention holds:
  pair\_01\_c1\_t1.mid - pair 1:  16 dyads did the experiment, 4 conditions (5 if you could each candidate leading as a
                                  different condition), and 4 trials per condition. One file for each candidates audio
                                  - in total 5*4*2=40 files per dyad at each tempo. Therefore each folder has 40 files.
                                  Can be pair\_01 - pair\_16.
                       
                       - c1: Candidate one. Data for the first of member of the dyad. Can be c1 or c2

                       - t1: Trial one. Data for the dyads first trial for the condition. Can be t1-t4.
                             These numbers are arbitrary, doesn't seem important in the original data.

- self: Experiments where each candidate hears only themselves (uncoupled).

- other: Experiments where they hear each other.

- comp: Each candidate is tapping along with a metronome.

- leader\_follower\_1: Experiments where candidate 1 hears their own audio, candidate 2 hears the audio of 
                       candidate 1. Therefore candidate 1 is the leader.

- leader\_follower\_2: Experiments where candidate 2 hears their own audio, candidate 1 hears the audio of 
                       candidate 2. Therefore candidate 2 is the leader.
